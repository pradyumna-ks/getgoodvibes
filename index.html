Features

Built for Figma, powered by AI

GoodVibes creates production-quality design work inside your existing Figma workflow.

Figma-Native

Real Figma nodes — frames, text, components, variables. Not images. Not exports. Edit like anything else on the canvas.

AI-Powered

Choose from Claude 4, DeepSeek, or GPT-4. Each model brings its own strengths to your design workflow.

shadcn + Lucide

Beautiful defaults: shadcn components, Lucide icons (200,000+ on demand), Inter typography. Or bring your own design system.

Smart Memory

Remembers your style preferences, naming conventions, and brand tokens across sessions. Every generation gets better.
